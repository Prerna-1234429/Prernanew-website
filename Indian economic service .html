Overview

The Indian Engineering Services (IES), also known as the Engineering Services Examination (ESE), is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) to recruit engineers for various technical and managerial roles in the Indian government. This exam is highly competitive and offers a prestigious career in public sector engineering.

Eligibility

To be eligible for the Indian Economic Service, candidates must fulfill the following requirements:

  • Nationality: Must be an Indian citizen.
  • Age Limit: Between 21 to 30 years with age relaxations to the reserved categories.

Syllabus

The IES syllabus covers core engineering disciplines and general studies, including:

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering
  • General Studies and Engineering Aptitude

Exam Pattern

The IES exam consists of three stages:

  • Stage 1: Preliminary Examination (Objective-type)
  • Stage 2: Main Examination (Descriptive-type)
  • Stage 3: Personality Test / Interview
